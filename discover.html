Start your Journey

How it Works

1
Sign Up
Visit our website and click on "Get Started", Click on “Sign Up”.
Then provide your email, choose a username, and set a
password. You'll receive a verification email; click the link to
activate your account.
2
Choose Your Game
Once logged in, browse our game library. We offer a wide
range of genres from action-packed adventures to strategic
puzzles. Select a game to see its rules, modes, and entry
requirements.
3
Enter Competitions
Check out the 'Competitions' tab for upcoming tournaments
and challenges. You can enter based on your skill level and
interest. Some competitions are free, while others require a
buy-in.
4
Win Rewards
Compete and climb the leaderboard to win rewards.
Rewards vary from in-game items, cash prizes, to exclusive
experiences. Your winnings are directly added to your
account, where you can withdraw or use them for future
competitions.
